Our Community Impact

Chaksu Haveli embodies the Rawat Family’s "One Village, One Product" philosophy, dedicated to creating sustainable livelihoods and preserving local heritage. At Chaksu Haveli, we believe in meaningful change through community empowerment. Over the years, we have employed more than 120 local residents in restoring the haveli, from crafting traditional plaster to preserving intricate architectural details. Today, trained villagers play essential roles in daily operations, from housekeeping to hospitality, showcasing their skills and cultural pride.

We are especially proud of our initiative to upskill women across the village in kantha embroidery, enabling them to contribute their artistry from home. By providing a platform for local folk artists, we celebrate Rajasthan’s rich cultural heritage and offer new opportunities for growth. With tourism in Chaksu village projected to increase, we remain committed to fostering a thriving local economy grounded in tradition and community spirit. Every guest experience contributes directly to this vision, helping to uplift and celebrate the people and culture that make Chaksu Haveli unique.
