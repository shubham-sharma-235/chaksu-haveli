Our Other Experiences

Farm-to-Table Dining

Enjoy a fresh, organic meal inspired by local
Rajasthani cuisine, grown on-site and prepared
using age-old culinary techniques. This immersive
dining experience is rooted in sustainability
and tradition.

Art and Craft Workshops

Participate in hands-on activities such
as pottery, ans and take home a piece of
Rajasthani culture you created yourself.

Wellness and Relaxation

Unwind with traditional wellness treatments, yoga
sessions, or meditation in the serene surroundings
of Chaksu Haveli, connecting with the natural
and cultural beauty of the space.

Star-Gazing

Spend a night under a blanket of stars, observing
famous constellations through a centuries-old
telescope. Experience the quiet majesty of
Rajasthan’s open skies as you connect with
the cosmos.

Bird-Watching

Visit Chandelai in the early hours to spot a
variety of migratory and indigenous birds in
their natural habitat. Enjoy a peaceful
morning immersed in the beauty of
Rajasthan’s wildlife.
