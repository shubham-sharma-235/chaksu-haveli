About Chaksu Haveli

Chaksu Haveli is a home away from home, nestled at the crossroads of culture, heritage, and luxury. We are a community of simple people, seeking connection, inspiration, and awareness. Our experience is powered by music, wellness, art, adventure, food, learning, science, and giving back. Stimulating all five senses, we curate a magical world of discovery and rejuvenation—a place that nourishes your soul and introduces you to a unique ethos shaped by centuries of tradition. Our rich heritage makes Chaksu Haveli peerless, offering an experience you won't find anywhere else. This is where you can reconnect with your inner creativity, finding a deeper sense of self and purpose.

Signature Experiences

Our signature experiences embody our distinctive ethos, core values, and curated programs, offering guests the very best of what our home has to offer.

Art Residency

Chaksu Haveli’s art residency program welcomes creators of all disciplines for a 3-4 month immersive stay in Rajasthan's culturally rich environment. Here, artists have the freedom to explore and develop their craft, culminating in a special exhibition that celebrates their journey and creations.

Chaksu Cultural Centre & Cultural Tour

Immerse yourself in Chaksu's artistic heritage with our Chaksu Cultural Centre & Cultural Tour, featuring traditional art, live performances, and a captivating light and sound show that unveils the history of Chaksu Haveli. Visit local artisans to experience the rich cultural legacy firsthand.

Day in the Life: Immersive Village Tour

Experience the heart of Chaksu with our Day in the Life: Immersive Village Tour, where you'll join a local guide to live, work, and connect with villagers in a journey through daily rural traditions.

Chaksu Essentials

Our Essentials section covers everything you need for a comfortable stay, from premium amenities to personalized services. Experience seamless hospitality that caters to every detail of your visit.

Our Manifesto

We believe the world needs a break—a
place that nurtures the soul, celebrates
simplicity and luxury, embraces culture
with a modern edge, and offers a life
rooted in purpose and heritage.

Our History

Chaksu Haveli, near the historic Talao,
showcases Rajasthani grandeur and heritage,
evolving from a Jain merchant hub to a
restored symbol of the past.

Our Restoration

The nine-year restoration of Chaksu Haveli
by Manglam Arts carefully preserves its
heritage with traditional Rajasthani
techniques, embodying the region’s
architectural legacy.

Our Sustainability

Chaksu Haveli offers an eco-conscious
experience rooted in sustainability
and cultural preservation, showcasing
local craftsmanship and authentic
Rajasthani flavors.

Our Community Impact

Chaksu Haveli exemplifies the Rawat Family’s
"One Village, One Product" philosophy,
empowering the community and promoting
sustainable livelihoods while honoring
Rajasthan's heritage.
