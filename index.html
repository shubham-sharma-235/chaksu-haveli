About Chaksu Haveli

Chaksu Haveli is a home away from home, nestled at the crossroads of culture, heritage, and luxury. We are a community of simple people, seeking connection, inspiration, and awareness. Our experience is powered by music, wellness, art, adventure, food, learning, science, and giving back. Stimulating all five senses, we curate a magical world of discovery and rejuvenation—a place that nourishes your soul and introduces you to a unique ethos shaped by centuries of tradition. Our rich heritage makes Chaksu Haveli peerless, offering an experience you won't find anywhere else. This is where you can reconnect with your inner creativity, finding a deeper sense of self and purpose.

Signature Experiences

Our signature experiences embody our distinctive ethos, core values, and curated programs, offering guests the very best of what our home has to offer.

Art Residency

At Chaksu Haveli, our art residency program invites creative individuals from all over the world—whether you’re an artist, musician, dancer, or creator of any discipline. We offer a unique space to explore your craft in a setting rich with history and inspiration. Artists are invited to apply for a 3-4 month stay, where they have complete freedom to create in any form or style. Whether you choose to embark on a guided journey of artistic discovery or prefer to be the captain of your own creative soul, we provide the environment and support to let your art flourish. At the end of the residency, your work will be featured in a special exhibition, allowing you to share your creations with the world. This exclusive program is designed to nurture artistic expression across all mediums, offering a sanctuary where creativity thrives in harmony with the cultural essence of Rajasthan.

Chaksu Cultural Centre & Cultural Tour

Explore the heart of Chaksu's vibrant artistic heritage with our curated Chaksu Cultural Centre & Cultural Tour. This experience takes you through the haveli’s art room, where traditional Rajasthani art is on display, followed by live musical performances that evoke the spirit of the region. As night falls, a mesmerizing light and sound show will unfold, narrating the history of Chaksu Haveli. Continue your journey with visits to local artisans, including tattoo artists, dancers, and women working at kantha looms, showcasing their intricate crafts passed down through generations. This tour offers a structured yet deeply immersive glimpse into the artistic and cultural legacy of Chaksu village.

Day in the Life: Immersive Village Tour

For those seeking a personal connection with the community, our Day in the Life: Immersive Village Tour offers a one-of-a-kind experience. Choose a local village informant to be your guide for the day, and immerse yourself in the rhythms of rural life from sunrise to sunset. Participate in daily activities like farming, cooking, crafting, and engaging in community traditions. This intimate cultural exchange allows you to live, work, and connect with the villagers, providing a profound and authentic perspective on life in Chaksu. It’s a journey into the heart of the village, where you’ll experience the simple beauty of life as it’s lived by the people of Chaksu.
