Day in the Life: Immersive Village Tour

For those seeking a personal connection with the community, our Day in the Life: Immersive Village Tour offers a one-of-a-kind experience. Choose a local village informant to be your guide for the day, and immerse yourself in the rhythms of rural life from sunrise to sunset. Participate in daily activities like farming, cooking, crafting, and engaging in community traditions. This intimate cultural exchange allows you to live, work, and connect with the villagers, providing a profound and authentic perspective on life in Chaksu. It’s a journey into the heart of the village, where you’ll experience the simple beauty of life as it’s lived by the people of Chaksu.
